Elon Musk gives dedication its meaning; a visionary who lives in the future and gives every waking moment he has to bringing it to fruition. Born on June 28th, 1971 in Pretoria, Transvaal, South Africa, Elon has a younger brother and sister. At the age of 17 Elon was accepted into Queen's University in Kingston for undergraduate study. In 1992, after spending several years at Queen's he transferred to Pennsylvania; he got a degree in physics and economics.

In 1995 Musk and his brother Kimbal started Zip2 - an internet based 'city guide' for the newspaper publishing industry. Compaq acquired Zip2 for US$307 million in cash and US$34 million in stock during February 1999. Elon Musk walked away with $22 million for his 7% share.

A month later, Musk co-founded X.com, an online financial services and e-mail payment company with US$10 million from the sale of Zip2. One year later, the company merged with PayPal. Musk was ousted in October 2000 from his role as CEO due to a disagreement with other company leaders. In October 2002 Paypal was acquired by eBay for US$1.5 billion in stock. Elon Musk made US$165 million.

After a series of explorations around a mission to mars, Elon Musk founded SpaceX.
And then Tesla.
And then SolarCity.
Elon Musk is a genius serial entrepreneur worth US$17 billion. He wants to make Human's a multi-planetary species before his death; and his plan to do it is incredible.
